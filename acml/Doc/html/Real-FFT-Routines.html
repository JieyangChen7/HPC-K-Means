5.3 FFTs on Real and Hermitian Data Sequences

The routines documented here compute discrete Fourier transforms (DFTs) of sequences of real numbers or of Hermitian sequences in either single or double precision arithmetic. The DFTs are computed using a highly-efficient FFT algorithm. Hermitian sequences are represented in one of the two formats that is described in Introduction to FFTs. The DFT of a real sequence results in a Hermitian sequence; the DFT of a Hermitian sequence is a real sequence.

Please note that prior to Release 2.0 of ACML the routine ZDFFT/CSFFT and ZDFFTM/CSFFTM returned results that were scaled by a factor 0.5 compared with the currently returned results.

  • 1D Real-To-Complex FFT (Hermitian-Packed Storage): One-dimensional real-to-complex transform (Hermitian-packed output)
  • Multiple 1D Real-To-Complex FFT (Hermitian-Packed Storage): Multiple one-dimensional real-to-complex transform (Hermitian-packed output)
  • 1D Complex-To-Real FFT (Hermitian-Packed Storage): One-dimensional complex-to-real transform (Hermitian-packed input)
  • Multiple 1D Complex-To-Real FFT (Hermitian-Packed Storage): Multiple one-dimensional complex-to-real transform (Hermitian-packed input)
  • 1D Real-To-Complex FFT (Complex-Hermitian Storage): One-dimensional real-to-complex transform (complex-Hermitian output)
  • 1D Complex-To-Real FFT (Complex-Hermitian Storage): One-dimensional complex-to-real transform (complex-Hermitian input)
  • Multiple 1D Real-To-Complex FFT (Complex-Hermitian Storage): Multiple one-dimensional real-to-complex transforms (complex-Hermitian output)
  • Multiple 1D Complex-To-Real FFT (Complex-Hermitian Storage): Multiple one-dimensional complex-to-real transforms (complex-Hermitian input)
  • 2D Real-To-Complex FFT: Two-dimensional real-to-complex transform (complex-Hermitian output)
  • 2D Complex-To-Real FFT: Two-dimensional complex-to-real transform (complex-Hermitian input)
  • 3D Real-To-Complex FFT: Three-dimensional real-to-complex transform (complex-Hermitian output)
  • 3D Complex-To-Real FFT: Three-dimensional complex-to-real transform (complex-Hermitian input)
